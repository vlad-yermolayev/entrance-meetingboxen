Entrance office pods in PCon

PCon is a specialized software program used for space planning and 3D visualization. The versatile product portfolio of Entrance Meetingboxen offers you the opportunity to give a project exactly the right message. And now, the furnishings are also available in pCon.planner, pCon.box, and pCon.facts.

pCon.planner

The intuitive and user-friendly pCon.planner is used worldwide by countless interior professionals. This 3D interior design software is available for laptops and computers, allowing you to utilize the Entrance meeting boxes in three-dimensional floor plans and spaces. Of course, it is also possible to export (parts of) the designed interior.

  • pCon.box

    For on-site use, pCon.box is an ideal tool. This app allows you to configure furniture and place it in the existing space using augmented reality. This is, of course, a great way to demonstrate how the products from Entrance Meetingboxen fit into a room. Additionally, pCon.box offers the ability to export images and configurations, for example as a PDF or via email or WhatsApp.

  • pCon.facts

    From photos to product information, pCon.facts contains all possible information about the products from Entrance Meetingboxen in pCon. It is also possible to configure products and place them in a space using augmented reality within pCon.facts. So, itâ€™s an ideal tool for on-the-go use.
